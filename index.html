About Us

More than a dance movement.

Young City Foundation is a home of hope, discipline, creativity, and transformation — where young people discover their voice, strengthen their character, and rise together toward a brighter future.

Our Mission

To empower young people through dance, mentorship, and education — giving them safe spaces to grow their talents, build confidence, and discover their purpose.

Our Vision

A world where every young person, no matter their background, has the opportunity to rise, shine, and succeed through creativity and discipline.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Neville Nyamongo at just 11 years old, Young City Crew began with one dream: to give children and teenagers a safe space to express themselves, grow their talents, and believe in their future.

Today, the foundation has grown into a strong and loving community with over 100 kids and two active hubs — one in Kibera and another in Kabiria. We train every week, Monday to Friday from 4 PM to 9 PM, building skill, confidence, focus, and discipline.

What makes Young City special is that we don't just dance — we teach discipline, love, and responsibility. Every member learns that talent is powerful, but it becomes truly meaningful when used to inspire and uplift others.

Foundation Coordinator

Felix Mboya

Felix Mboya is a dedicated youth leader, mentor, and community development professional serving as the Foundation Coordinator of the organization. With a deep passion for empowering young people, Felix plays a central role in overseeing programs that nurture talent, build discipline, and create opportunities for growth in dance, football, rugby, beadwork, and visual arts such as drawing and design.

As Foundation Coordinator, Felix is responsible for managing daily operations, coordinating program leaders, building partnerships, and ensuring that every initiative aligns with the foundation’s mission of transforming lives through talent, creativity, sports, and education. His leadership focuses on creating safe spaces where children and young people can discover their abilities, develop confidence, and become responsible members of society.

Felix strongly believes that talent is a powerful tool for change. Through his work, he has helped connect youth to mentorship, training opportunities, competitions, and community projects that encourage both personal and professional growth. His vision is to build a generation of young leaders who use their gifts not only for success, but also to uplift their communities.

Known for his strong organizational skills, commitment to excellence, and heart for service, Felix Mboya continues to be a driving force behind the foundation’s mission of empowering youth through movement, sports, creativity, and life skills development.
